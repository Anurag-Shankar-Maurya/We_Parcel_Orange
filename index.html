Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

You can track your parcel using the tracking number provided in your confirmation email. Simply enter this number in the tracking section of our website or mobile app. Alternatively, you can track using your mobile number, AWB, Order ID, or LRN.

Our standard delivery hours are from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM, seven days a week. For specific time-slot deliveries, you can select your preferred time during the booking process, subject to availability.

Delivery costs are calculated based on distance, package dimensions, weight, and the type of service selected (same-day, next-day, etc.). You can get an instant quote by entering your pickup and delivery locations on our website or app.

All parcels shipped with We Parcel are insured against damage or loss. In the unlikely event of an issue, please contact our customer support team within 7 days of the delivery date. We'll investigate and process your claim promptly.

Yes, you can reschedule your delivery through our website or mobile app. Go to the tracking section, enter your tracking details, and select the reschedule option. Please note that rescheduling should be done at least 3 hours before the scheduled delivery time.

Yes, we offer international shipping to over 100 countries worldwide. Rates and delivery times vary by destination. You can get a quote and book international shipments through our website or by contacting our customer service team.
